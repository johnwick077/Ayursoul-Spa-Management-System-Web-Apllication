Facial Treatments

Classic Facial

₹799.00

A relaxing, deep-cleansing facial that includes cleansing, exfoliation, extractions, and a mask. It is suitable for all skin types and aims to refresh and rejuvenate the skin.

Anti-Aging Facial

₹1499.00

This facial targets fine lines, wrinkles, and age spots, using specialized products and techniques to stimulate collagen production and improve skin elasticity. It often includes ingredients like retinol, peptides, and hyaluronic acid.

Hydrating Facial

₹999.00

Ideal for dry or dehydrated skin, this treatment focuses on restoring moisture and nourishing the skin. It uses hydrating masks and serums to leave the skin soft, plump, and glowing.

Acne Facial

₹1199.00

Designed for acne-prone skin, this facial involves deep cleansing, exfoliation, and extractions. It often uses antibacterial or anti-inflammatory ingredients like salicylic acid and tea tree oil to reduce breakouts and prevent future ones.

Deep Tissue Massage

₹1699.00

This massage technique is more intense and targets deeper layers of muscles and connective tissues. It is especially helpful for chronic aches and pains, focusing on knots, stiff neck, and upper back pain.

Swedish Massage

₹1399.00

A gentle, relaxing massage that focuses on soothing and relieving muscle tension. It uses long, smooth strokes, kneading, and circular movements on the topmost layers of muscles, perfect for relaxation and stress relief.

Hot Stone Massage

₹1299.00

Heated stones are placed on specific points on the body and used in the massage to warm up tight muscles and improve blood flow. The heat helps relieve muscle tension and allows for a deeper, more relaxing experience.

Aromatherapy Massage

₹1899.00

This massage combines gentle pressure with the use of essential oils. Each essential oil offers unique benefits, from relaxation to energy boost. The combination of scent and massage is calming and can help relieve stress and anxiety.

Shirodhara

₹2399.00

In this treatment, warm herbal oil is gently poured onto the forehead, typically onto the "third eye" area. It’s deeply relaxing, reduces stress, improves mental clarity, and can aid in relieving conditions like insomnia, headaches, and anxiety.

Abhyanga

₹1499.00

A full-body massage using warm herbal oils, Abhyanga is designed to balance the doshas, nourish the skin, and improve circulation. The oil selection varies based on individual needs and body type, promoting relaxation, detoxification, and rejuvenation.

Pizhichil

₹1599.00

Also known as "herbal oil bath," this therapy involves pouring warm, medicated oils over the entire body in a rhythmic motion. It combines the benefits of Abhyanga with a constant stream of warm oil, providing pain relief, improving skin health, and rejuvenating the body.

Udvartana

₹1399.00

This invigorating herbal scrub massage is used for exfoliation and to reduce excess fat or "kapha" in the body. The treatment involves scrubbing the skin with herbal powders or pastes, improving circulation, enhancing skin tone, and promoting detoxification.

Body Scrub

₹1499.00

A body scrub removes dead skin cells, leaving the skin smooth and refreshed. This treatment typically uses exfoliating ingredients like sugar, salt, or coffee grounds, combined with essential oils, to rejuvenate the skin.

Body Wrap

₹1499.00

Body wraps use ingredients such as clay, seaweed, or herbal-infused wraps that are applied to the skin to detoxify, hydrate, or slim. After application, the body is wrapped in a warm layer to help the treatment penetrate the skin.

Hydrotherapy

₹1699.00

Hydrotherapy treatments use water in different forms—like steam baths, saunas, and whirlpool baths—to relax muscles, relieve tension, and improve circulation.

Mud Therapy

₹1799.00

Mud therapy involves applying mineral-rich mud to the body, which can help in soothing skin, detoxifying the body, and alleviating joint or muscle pain. This is especially beneficial for skin rejuvenation.
